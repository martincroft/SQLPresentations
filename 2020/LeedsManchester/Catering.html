Catering Options

Explore our catering choices

LittleVegan can provide a variety of food types for all your catering needs. We offer afternoon teas, cold and hot buffets, burger bar etc. We are based in Leyland Lancashire and offer cruelty free, guilt free food, using fresh ingredients locally sourced where possible. We can cater for events such as christenings/baby naming days family get-togethers, birthdays, anniversaries or what ever your celebration or get-together is.
Drop us an email/message/call to discuss further.
